Learn Git!

One of the most famous and widely used revision control system in the world!

Why use Git?

  • New generation of revision control systems
  • Open source
  • Designed and implemented by Linus Torvalds, the creator of Linux
  • Each day it's market share is increasing. Now it is roughly 40%
  • Supported by all major IDEs, including Visual Studio, Eclipse, IntelliJ Idea and various others
  • Easy branching model
  • Various tools built around Git to support code reviewing
  • Proven workflows that support concern separation
  • Many other powerful features that streamline team cooperation

What will you find on this site?

A theoretical introduction to git. As your knowledge and understanding of git grows larger you can see the Advanced section and learn more elaborate things. There's also a simple practical workshop prepared to review what you have learned.
